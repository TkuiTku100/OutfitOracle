Welcome to the website


Welcome to Outfit Oracle, your personalized fashion assistant that blends style with convenience to curate outfits tailored just for you.

At Outfit Oracle, we understand that choosing the perfect outfit can be a daunting task, especially when you have to consider the ever-changing weather and your unique style preferences.

That's why we've created a platform that does the hard work for you, ensuring you step out in confidence, no matter the occasion or weather forecast.

After choosing weather condition, temperature, and time, you can generate your outfit by pressing on the 'generate' button.
If you are not pleased with the outcome, you can re-generate or change every part of the suggested outfit, we'll learn from it and do better next time ;)
Once you're done the system will assume you liked the outfit.
